🧾 GST Guru – Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last Updated: April 1, 2025

📋 Introduction

Welcome to GST Guru, a GST calculator and GSTIN verification app developed by educationhub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

By using GST Guru, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

📦 Information We Collect

  • GST Search History — We store your recent GST/HSN code searches locally on your device (offline, Room Database). This data never leaves your device.
  • Camera & Gallery Access — When you use the OCR scanner feature, we access your camera or gallery to scan GST-related documents. Images are processed on-device and are NOT uploaded to any server.
  • Advertising Data — We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No Personal Registration — We do not require account creation, login, name, or password.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do NOT share your search history with third parties
  • We do NOT upload camera/gallery images to any server
  • We do NOT have any user accounts or registration system
  • We do NOT sell your data to anyone

🔒 App Permissions Explained

  • INTERNET — Required for GSTIN verification API and loading ads
  • CAMERA — Required for OCR scanner to scan GST documents
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / MEDIA_IMAGES — Required to pick images from gallery for OCR scanning
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to send GST filing reminders (optional)
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Required for accurate GST reminder notifications

📢 Google AdMob & Advertising

GST Guru uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including but not limited to:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address and general location
  • Ad interaction and performance data
📌 Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

💾 Data Storage & Security

  • All search history is stored locally on your device using Room Database
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from Settings
  • We use industry-standard security practices for all API calls (HTTPS)

👶 Children's Privacy

GST Guru is a professional GST utility app intended for use by adults and business professionals. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
